我没有朋友 / 我爱快

It was a rather normal day at the FBM racetrack, but it surely was not for Mr. Manuel. Another race was going to occur this day, but Mr. Manuel, the track supervisor, was homesick. Being that the racetrack's viewer rates are dropping, it was extremely important that the race still goes on, and that it goes well. After having looked through the crowd, the FBM staff has come to the conclusion that you are the only one with enough experience to run the race. It is up to you to use the racetrack control panel to keep it going, and keep it interesting. The future careers of the racetrack workers and drivers depends on you, do not let them down, and good luck.
